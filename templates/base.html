Story about me

I am lucky, because I combine my favorite business and work. I've been drawing since I was a child and I've never seen myself in anything else. Each illustration for me is a new story told by me. All my characters resemble the characters of a children's fairy tale. This is not just a coincidence, because in the future I dream of illustrating books for children. What could be more wonderful and carefree than the good old fairy tales told to us as children?;)
